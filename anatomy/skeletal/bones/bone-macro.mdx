# Bone Anatomy: Macroscopic parts

<span class="accent">Red bone marrow</span> contains blood stem cells for hematopoiesis, the production of (all) blood cells. 
<span class="accent">Yellow marrow</span> primarily stores fat, but also contains stem cells that can become cartilage, fat, or bone cells.
